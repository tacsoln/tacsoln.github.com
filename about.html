About Us

Maritime Tactical Solutions (MTS) is a tactical securities firm headquartered in Washington, D.C., Founded in 2006 by visionary executives, all of them former Navy Seals, Elite Special Forces, or special operations and intelligence professionals trained to meet specialized requirements.  As a global leader in security-related services for commercial and government nautical vessels. We have a highly qualified and capable organization. With teams ‘on the ready’ our services include armored chaperones, transportation of collateral, long-distance transport of valuables, vaulting and other high end security solutions. With many years of dedicated service, we serve customers around the world and have a long standing reputation for excellence. Our expertise comes from experience. We continue to move forward as a powerful securities firm by developing strong client relationships based on efficiency, effectiveness, positive communication, trust, and discretion.
